Kathakali

Kathakali is an evolved dance form popular in the South Indian state of Kerala. This Indian classical dance involves storytelling activities through fascinating footwork and expressive gestures of face and hands accompanied by music and vocal performance.

Vallam Kali

Vallam Kali (vaḷḷaṃ kaḷi, literally: boat game), also known as Snake Boat Race, is a traditional boat race
in Kerala, India. It is a form of canoe racing, and uses paddled war canoes.
